Disney princess

Are they good role models for young children?

The question that we wanted to explore was whether Disney Princesses were appropriate role models for young girls. We were, as a group, very interested and curious about the idea because we had heard about the negative body image portrayal and wanted to evaluate the appropriateness of these 'role models.'
